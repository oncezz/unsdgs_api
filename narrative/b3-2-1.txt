Welcome back to our online course: Trade and the Sustainable Development Goals.<br><br>
In this module, we examine the relationship between trade, productivity and innovation.<br><br>
Sustainable Development Goal 9 addresses infrastructure, technology development, and innovation. This module will discuss how trade can influence productivity and innovation. It will discuss the productivity-enhancing effects of trade and the complementarities between trade, FDI, and efforts to attain this and other Sustainable Development Goals.<br><br>
